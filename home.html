Finnian Fleming is a computer science student that has been studying computer science for 4 years, starting from Penair School through to the University of Aberdeen. He has achievements such as an 8 in GCSE Maths, Alevels in Maths, Physics and Computer Science, and a second place in Software Cornwall's Mars Rover competition.

He is currently based in Aberdeen where he studies computing science at the University of Aberdeen and is in his first year. He has averaged a first in his first semester and is on track for a first in his first year at the University, with modules such as Web development, OOP, and electives in Maths, Finance, and Philosophy. Finn was born in Hackney, London but grew up in Truro, Cornwall, where he attended Kea Primary School, Penair School, and Truro College.

Finn achieved GCSE results of an 8 in maths and a 7 in English among others, which let him study A levels in Maths, Physics, and Computer Science at Truro College, where he graduated in 2022 and went on a gap year, working locally at Dunelm before starting at the University of Aberdeen.

Finn has been part of many extracurricular groups such as Beavers, Cubs, and Scouts in primary school receiving the Gold Scouts award, before moving on to play Basketball for Penryn Saracens and then for Cornwall Saracens at u14 and u16 levels, winning most improved player for his first season with the u16, then joining the Truro College basketball team.

He placed second in the Software Cornwall Mars Rover competition while at Penair School and received the Sports Leader award while attending Penair School for completing the course in his penultimate year.
